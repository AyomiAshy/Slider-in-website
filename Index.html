Attack on titan

Attack on titan is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Hajime Isayama. It is set in a world where humanity lives in cities surrounded by three enormous walls to protect them from gigantic man-eating humanoids called Titans. The story follows Eren Yeager, who vows to exterminate the Titans after they destroy his hometown and kill his mother.In the series, the military uses Vertical Maneuvering Equipment (VME), also known as Omni-Directional Maneuvering Gear (ODM Gear), to combat Titans. This equipment is a set of waist-mounted grappling hooks and gas-powered propulsion that enables mobility in three dimensions. The story spans over 2000 years and involves various types of Titans, including Pure Titans, Abnormal Titans, and the Nine Titans, also known as Titan Shifters. Titan Shifters can shift between human and Titan form and possess the ability to harden certain parts of their bodies.

One piece

One piece is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Eiichiro Oda. It follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy, a young man who gains rubber-like powers after eating a Devil Fruit, as he sets out to become the Pirate King by finding the ultimate treasure, the "One Piece," left behind by the previous Pirate King, Gol D. Roger. Luffy's journey begins in the East Blue Sea, where he assembles his crew, the Straw Hat Pirates, which includes Roronoa Zoro, a skilled swordsman; Nami, a skilled navigator; Usopp, a marksman and storyteller; Sanji, a master chef and kickboxer; Tony Tony Chopper, a reindeer doctor; Nico Robin, an archaeologist; Franky, a cyborg shipwright; Brook, a musician and swordsman; and Jinbe, a fish-man and former Warlord of the Sea.

Solo Leveling

is a South Korean portal fantasy web novel written by Chugong and serialized in Kakao's digital comic and fiction platform KakaoPage starting on July 25, 2016. The story follows Sung Jinwoo, a notoriously weak E-Rank hunter who finds himself in a world where hunters, humans with supernatural abilities, must battle deadly monsters to protect mankind from certain annihilation. After narrowly surviving an overwhelmingly powerful double dungeon that nearly wipes out his entire party, a mysterious program called the System chooses him as its sole player and grants him the unique ability to level up in strength, something no other hunter can do. Jinwoo then sets out on a journey to discover the secrets of the dungeons and the true source of his powers as he fights against all kinds of enemies, both man and monster. The narrative explores themes of power, betrayal, and redemption, set against a backdrop of interdimensional warfare and cosmic manipulation.The story concludes with Jinwoo defeating the final antagonist, Antares, the King of Dragons and the Monarch of Destruction, and using the Holy Grail to turn back time, allowing him to spend 27 years in a temporal rift eradicating the Monarchs and their armies, effectively altering Earth's history. In the new reality, Jinwoo remains the sole bearer of immense power and chooses to stay on Earth, reencountering his schoolmate, Cha Hae-In, hinting at a semblance of normalcy and peace in a world once rife with conflict and chaos.
